Will it fit?

Measuring yourself for clothing is an important step to ensure that you get the right fit and size when shopping online or in-store. To get started, you will need a measuring tape and a mirror.
First, measure your chest by placing the tape measure under your arms and around the fullest part of your chest. Next, measure your waist by wrapping the tape measure around the narrowest part of your waistline.
For hip measurements, place the tape measure around the widest part of your hips. For sleeve length, measure from the center of your back to your wrist bone.
Finally, for inseam measurements, measure from the crotch to the bottom of your ankle. With these measurements, you can confidently shop for clothing that fits you perfectly.
